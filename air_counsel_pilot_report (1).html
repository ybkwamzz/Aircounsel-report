Qualification Methodology
Every inbound enquiry is scored across six gates derived from UK Immigration Rules: Route fit (mapped to firm practice areas) · Eligibility thresholds (Skilled Worker £38,700 / New Entrant £30,960; Spouse Visa £29,000 post-April 2024; ILR 180-day absence limit) · Immigration history (prior refusals flagged as complex/high-fee, not discarded) · Fee capacity (employment status, employer funding, Legal Aid filter) · Jurisdiction (catchment and consultation method) · Urgency (deadline days to priority band). Scores below 45 are redirected automatically. Scores above 75 trigger a priority callback alert. No partner time is used on non-viable enquiries.
